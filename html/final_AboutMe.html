Get To Know Me

Hey there! My name is Anoosha Syed and I’m just your average 20-year-old who wishes there was a good way to balance college, work, and travel. Although, I haven’t traveled as much as the typical travel blogger, I still would like to document my experiences to the places I have been mainly for my own memories as well as anyone who finds visiting nature and exploring various cultures interesting.

Travelling to different places and experiencing different cultures and customs has always been something that I was very drawn to as a young child. I think coming from a family who originated from Pakistan, which is a country that in itself has so many amazing places to travel and so much culture to learn from, also played a big part in discovering this passion of mine.
